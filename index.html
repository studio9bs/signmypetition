A story driven beat em up at mouse-scale. Crush enemies, discover mouse tribes, and collect signatures from tenants. In a world where ant gangs rule the hallways, nightmares can be fought, and mice are forced to work as appliances.
The Chosen One, champion of his mouse tribe, must stop the Exterminator. He travels through a crumbling apartment building collecting ritual signatures from human tenants. To earn them, he battles gangs of ants, deals with eccentric residents, and meets other mouse tribes each struggling with their own strange problems.


Key Features

Ridiculous weapons of questionable proportions 

Why are hammers huge and the flamethrowers tiny? Don’t worry about it. Just use them. There will be plenty of weapons.

Unique characters, places, and stories 

40+ hand-drawn residents with personalities, problems, and weird moments you’ll stumble into.

Reactions both good and bad 

Most characters can be talked to or interacted with in simple ways. They’ll react differently depending on what you do and some don’t forget. So… try not to hand out too many black eyes.

Power-ups shake up combat 

Find and buy items that will power-up certain attacks. Hot sauce makes kicks explosive. Roach milk, as expected, super-charges your uppercut.
