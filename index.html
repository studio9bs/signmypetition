Studio 9Bs presents - Loony hijinks, story-driven brawling, mice, mayhem, and martial arts.
When his sick human friend Boe faces eviction and the mice of his tribe are threatened with extermination, one tiny hero rises to stop it all. As the Chosen One, you’ll brawl your way through a crumbling apartment building overrun by bumbling mice, scheming ants, and increasingly ridiculous money-making plots. Each apartment is a new fight, a new tenant to convince, and another signature to collect. Punch bugs, take on oddball quests, and lead the most peculiar, and misguided revolution this building’s ever seen.


Key Features

Story-Driven Brawling 

Throw tiny fists in big fights across apartments filled with ants, beetles, and other weirdos.

Apartment-Scale Adventure 

Each unit is its own world complete with tenants, side quests, secrets, and signature-worthy struggles.

A Revolution in Petitions 

Collect signatures from reluctant neighbors in your quest to stop an exterminator and maybe save your sick best friend.

Light RPG Mechanics 

Choose your attacks, complete quests, and lead the Chosen One on a path through bugs and bureaucracy.

Satirical World, Sincere Heart 

A tale of friendship, resistance, and dumb ideas with a surprising amount of emotional punch.

Bugs With Agendas 

Uncover a hidden network of ant-run schemes, shady businesses, and strange operations all part of a larger scheme buried just beneath the floorboards.
We’re a small team of seasoned software developers turned indie game devs, building weird worlds. After years of making ed-tech and enterprise software, we decided to make something a little more... unpredictable. Our games lean into the absurd, but behind the chaos is a tight, experienced team that knows how to get things done.
