Studio 9Bs presents - Sign My Petition
A story driven beat em up at mouse-scale. Crush enemies, discover mouse tribes, and collect signatures from tenants. In a world where ant gangs rule the hallways, nightmares can be fought, and mice are forced to work as appliances.
Boe is sick and facing eviction. His only friends are the tribe of mice who live in his apartment who are themselves facing extermination. In desperation, the mice send their hero, the Chosen One, on a dangerous quest to rally the building’s tenants and save them all.
But the world beyond Boe’s walls is treacherous. Reluctant tenants, hostile ants, and bizarre money-making schemes stand in the way. To succeed, he will have to brawl, bargain, and uncover a deeper plot lurking behind the eviction notice.
As the Chosen One, you’ll fight through a crumbling apartment building overrun by bumbling mice, power-hungry bugs, and eccentric neighbors. Each apartment is a new battleground, another signature to win, a new obstacle to outsmart. Every victory inching you closer to the truth of what’s happening here.


Key Features

Story-Driven Brawling 

Throw tiny fists in big fights across apartments filled with ants, beetles, and other weirdos.

Apartment-Scale Adventure 

Each unit is its own world complete with tenants, side quests, secrets, and signature-worthy struggles.

A Revolution in Petitions 

Collect signatures from reluctant neighbors in your quest to stop an exterminator and maybe save your sick best friend.

Light RPG Mechanics 

Choose your attacks, complete quests, and lead the Chosen One on a path through bugs and bureaucracy.

Satirical World, Sincere Heart 

A tale of friendship, resistance, and dumb ideas with a surprising amount of emotional punch.

Bugs With Agendas 

Uncover a hidden network of ant-run schemes, shady businesses, and strange operations all part of a larger scheme buried just beneath the floorboards.
